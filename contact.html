About This Dashboard

This standalone dashboard provides interactive visualization of water quality data across Telangana state. The system is designed to make environmental monitoring data accessible and understandable for researchers, policymakers, and the general public.

Data Period

June 2025 - Latest available monitoring data

Coverage

  • 213 monitoring stations
  • Major rivers: Godavari, Krishna, Musi, Manjeera, Kinneresani
  • Urban and rural lakes, tanks, and reservoirs
  • 18 water quality parameters

Data Privacy & Usage

All data displayed in this dashboard is sourced from public monitoring reports published by the Telangana Pollution Control Board. The data is used for educational and awareness purposes.
